Some folks we’ve worked with:

The truth is you have a lot on your plate when it comes to running a successful agency…

You might be struggling with:

Figuring out why you're not that profitable

  • It’s unclear if it’s utilization, over-servicing, or something else.
  • Your team says they’re busy, your accountant says you’re overstaffed.
  • Even when you are busy, cash & profit aren’t great.

You just want to know what’s wrong, and what to do about it.

Forecasting people and profits

  • You cant easily predict who and when youll need to hire.
  • You’re struggling to predict project or client profitability.
  • Keeping your team busy enough, or avoiding overworking them is a challenge.

You just want to be able to plan ahead, but it feels harder than it should.

Trying to track the right stuff

  • Youre unsure what metrics to be tracking.
  • Youre not clear on what benchmarks to aim for.
  • Youre not sure if youre calculating metrics in the right way.
  • Youre having trouble getting the right data together from all your different tools.

You just want reports and data to answer your most important questions.

Cleaning up your messy operations tooling and data

  • Youre not sure why your time-tracking and project management tools are set up the way they are.
  • Reporting takes a lot of manual work & data cleanup.
  • Your tools and data feel disconnected, the same things are named or structured differently across tools.

You just want to know what tools to use and how to set them up to get the insights you need without a bunch of manual work.

Constantly having to fight against change

  • Changes to your agency’s tools, process, pricing, people and services breaks your reporting system.
  • You’re tired of changing tools and processes only to end up back where you started.
  • The team can never quite seem to follow the process or keep their inputs clean.
  • There are always little errors in the data that prevent you from truly automating reporting.

You just want to set up a process that’s actually going to work long-term and be scalable.

You want to fix it, but you’re not sure where to start…

Model Your Agency’s Performance

By the end of this process, you’ll have clarity on:

  • Exactly how much profit you’re losing and why.
  • How your performance compares to others.
